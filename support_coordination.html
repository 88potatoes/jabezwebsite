Support Coordination

Support coordination involves helping participants navigate and implement their NDIS plans. This includes understanding the plan, connecting with service providers, and ensuring that the funded supports align with the participant's goals.

How you can help

  • Provide clear explanations of the NDIS plan details.
  • Facilitate communication between the participant and various service providers.
  • Work collaboratively to implement and review the plan regularly.
  • Advocate for the participant's needs within the NDIS framework.
